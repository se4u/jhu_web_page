I, Pushpendre Rastogi, graduated from IIT Delhi in August 2011 with dual degrees in EE and IT. After graduation, I worked at Goldman Sachs as a Core Ops Strat for a year before joining Aspiring Minds as an RnD engineer in 2012. Since 2013, I have worked at The Center For Language and Speech Processing at the Johns Hopkins University as a CS Ph.D. student. Currently, I am interested in solving problems posted on StackOverflow.

At CLSP, my advisor is Benjamin Van Durme and I have worked with Raman Arora and Jason Eisner.
